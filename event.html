FINDING BIG WORD

Round 1: Mini-Game Phase Teams play quick games.Winners earn letters, distributed among their pairs. Losing teams risks elimination, with pairs who contribute nothing being removed. Round 2: Trading and Word Solving Phase Remaining pairs from both teams trade letters through tasks, with failure leading to elimination if they have no letters. Survivors then work together, pooling their letters, to guess a secret word. The first pair to correctly shout the word wins.
